Signing in

  1. Open the app link in a browser (works on phone, tablet or desktop).
  2. Enter your email and password and tap Login.
  3. You'll land on the home screen. The tiles and menu you see depend on your role — if a feature is missing, your role doesn't have access to it.

Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot password" link to receive a reset email (the link is valid for one hour).

Staying signed in: the app remembers you on the same device until you sign out. It signs you out automatically after your session expires (you'll be returned to the login screen).

Finding your way around

  • The home tiles and the sidebar menu both take you to any screen.
  • The ☰ menu button collapses the sidebar on desktop and opens a drawer on phones.
  • On screens with sub-sections (like Products or Staff), use the tabs at the top to switch between areas.
  • The 📊 Sheets button (when shown) opens the underlying Google Sheet for the current screen — handy for admins.

Creating a bill

  1. Go to Billing → New Bill.
  2. Type the customer's phone number to search.
    • If they exist, their name appears, along with their tier badge and Gem balance.
    • If not, register them on the spot with name + phone.
  3. (Optional) choose a Price Book to apply special pricing.
  4. Add line items with + Add Row. For each row:
    • Pick Service or Product, then the item.
    • Assign the staff member who performed/sold it (required).
    • Set the quantity. Price and GST fill in automatically; adjust if needed.
  5. Enter a Discount (₹ or %) and/or a Tip if applicable.
  6. If the customer wants to redeem Gems, see Redeeming Gems below.
  7. Choose the Payment Mode: Cash, Card, UPI, or Split (enter the amounts for each).
  8. Check the Grand Total and the "Gems will be earned" preview.
  9. Tap Save & Generate Invoice, confirm, then print or share the invoice.
If Save is greyed out You're missing something: a customer, a complete line item with a staff member and quantity, or (for Split payments) the amounts don't add up to the total.

Redeeming Gems

  1. Look the customer up first — their Gem balance must be visible.
  2. In the summary, enter the number of Gems to redeem.
  3. The system converts them to a rupee discount automatically (based on your configured conversion) and shows the amount off. You can't redeem more than the customer has.
  4. Finish the bill as usual. Redeemed Gems are deducted from the customer's balance — but their tier is not affected.

Viewing & voiding bills

  • Go to History to see recent bills (last 90 days by default).
  • Open a bill to review its items.
  • To cancel a bill, use Void. Important Voiding marks the bill cancelled but does not automatically return stock or reverse Gems — adjust those manually if required.

Appointments

  1. Go to Appointments. Use the day navigator (‹ Today ›) to move between days.
  2. Switch between List and Timeline views.
  3. Tap + New Appointment, choose the customer, staff, service, start time and duration, and save.
  4. Update an appointment's status as the visit progresses.

Expenses

  1. Go to Expenses.
  2. Add an expense with date, category, vendor, amount, payment mode and any reference number.
  3. Use the filters (period, category, payment mode) to review spending; the summary bar totals what's shown.
  4. Void an expense entered by mistake.

Services & service groups

Go to Services — three tabs:

  • Service Groups — create groups first. A group sets GST%, SAC code, whether it counts toward staff targets, a direct-incentive %, display order, and Eligible for loyalty points (tick this so services in the group earn Gems).
  • Service Catalog — add each service under a group, with its default price and duration.
  • Price Books — see below.

Products & stock

Go to Products — six tabs:

  • Product Groups — set GST%, HSN code, per-unit staff incentive, and Eligible for loyalty points.
  • Products — add each product: category (Retail or Professional), unit of measure, cost, retail price, GST, current stock, reorder level, manufacturer, vendor.
  • Purchase Orders / Receive Stock / Stock Register / Stock Audit — see below.

Ordering & receiving stock

  1. Purchase Orders → + Create PO: choose a vendor, add products with quantities and unit costs, save. The PO starts as draft.
  2. Receive Stock: pick "Against Purchase Order" (choose the PO) or "Direct Receipt" (choose a vendor). Enter quantities received and save — stock goes up and the PO advances to partial or received.
  3. Stock Register: the full history of every stock change; filter by product, type or date.
  4. Stock Audit: load products, enter the physical count, and save. The system records the variance and corrects the stock to your count.

Price books

  1. Under Services → Price Books, create a price book (e.g. "Weekend Pricing").
  2. Open it and set special prices for the services you want to override; the rest fall back to their default price.
  3. Select the price book at the top of a bill to apply it.

Setting up the Gems programme

  1. Go to Settings → Loyalty Programme.
  2. Tick Enable Loyalty Programme.
  3. Set the Points Name to Gems.
  4. Set the Base Earn Rate (Gems per ₹100 of eligible spend) and the Expiry in months.
  5. Under Redemption, set how many Gems equal ₹1 off, the value per unit, and the minimum Gems needed to redeem.
  6. In the Tiers table, name the four tiers, set each one's status-point threshold, earn multiplier, and badge colour. (The first tier always starts at 0.)
  7. Tap Save Loyalty Settings.
Remember A service or product only earns Gems if its group has "Eligible for loyalty points" ticked. Set that up in Services/Products first.

Running a happy hour

  • Right now: tick Active right now and set the multiplier — everyone earning during this time gets the bonus. Untick it to stop.
  • Recurring: add one or more windows — choose the day, the from/to times, and (optionally) an effective-from date. Happy hour turns on automatically during those windows.
  • Save your changes. During happy hour, the billing screen shows a 🎉 badge and the extra Gems in the earn preview.

Staff

  1. Go to Staff → Staff tab and add each team member: name, phone, role, salary, allowance, specialisation.
  2. Assign a Comp Plan and a target period (weekly or monthly).
  3. A staff member's portal PIN defaults to the last 4 digits of their phone until they change it.

Other Staff tabs: Comp Plans (define commission structures), Shifts (working-hour templates), Attendance, and Payroll.

Attendance & approvals

  • Recording attendance: managers can enter it directly under Staff → Attendance. Staff can self-log from their portal.
  • Each day is present, absent, or half-day, with clock-in/out times. Overtime counts beyond 9 hours a day.
  • Approving: go to HR Approvals → Attendance. Review staff-submitted (pending) records and Approve or Reject. Absent staff for the day are listed so you can record them.

Salary advances

  • Staff request advances from their portal (they enter a pending request).
  • In HR Approvals → Advance Requests: Approve with an amount, then Disburse with a payment mode. You can also Reject.
  • A running balance is maintained and deducted at payroll time.

Running payroll

  1. Go to Staff → Payroll.
  2. Choose the staff member and the month.
  3. Confirm the eligible free offs (default 4) and payable days (default 26).
  4. Calculate to see the breakdown: base salary, leave deduction, overtime pay, incentives, advances deducted, and net pay. (Weekend absences count double.)
  5. Review and Save the payroll. Update its status when paid.

Outlets & users

  • Organizations: build your company tree — the parent company and each outlet (Naturals, FirstCry) as child orgs. An org can't be deleted while it has children.
  • Users: add each staff/manager login with their name, email, role and outlet. Emails must be unique. A parent-org user sees all outlets beneath them; an outlet user sees only theirs.

Roles & permissions

  1. Roles: create roles like Manager, Cashier, etc.
  2. Permissions: pick a role, then tick which menu items it can access. Save. Users with that role will only see the allowed features.

Sheet setup & verification (admins)

  • Go to Settings → Sheet Setup & Verification. It checks that every required data sheet exists with the right columns.
  • If anything is Missing or has Missing columns, tick the items and press Run Setup to create/repair them automatically.
  • 📋 Refresh Summary Sheet rebuilds the "Index" tab in the Google Sheet — a friendly directory of all data tabs.

Customer portal ("My Gems")

Share the customer portal link with your customers. They:

  1. Enter their phone number — no password needed.
  2. See their tier, Gem balance, and progress toward the next tier.
  3. Browse their visit history, with the Gems earned or redeemed on each visit.

Staff portal

Share the staff portal link with your team. They log in with phone + PIN (default: last 4 digits of phone) and can:

  • Confirm the bill items credited to them (keeps incentives accurate).
  • Log attendance (clock in/out) — sent to their manager for approval.
  • Request an advance.
  • Change their PIN.

Troubleshooting

ProblemWhat to do
"Session expired" / kicked to loginJust log in again. Sessions time out after several hours for security.
Customer's Gems / tier not showing on a billMake sure you searched by their exact phone number and they're registered. The loyalty programme must be enabled in Settings.
A service/product isn't earning GemsIts group must have "Eligible for loyalty points" ticked.
Can't save a billAdd a customer, complete at least one line with a staff member and quantity, and (for Split) make the amounts total the grand total.
A feature is missing from my menuYour role doesn't have access. Ask an admin to grant it under Roles & Permissions.
Stock looks wrongRun a Stock Audit to reconcile to the physical count. Remember voiding a bill doesn't restore stock automatically.
Staff can't log in to the portalTheir phone must match their staff record; the default PIN is the last 4 digits of that phone.
Password reset email issuesReset links expire after one hour; request a new one if needed.
